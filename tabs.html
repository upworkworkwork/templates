The purpose of this computer application is to allow you to follow certain pedagogical information concerning you (justified absences, attendance exemptions, CC notes in the teaching units subject to full continuous monitoring, etc.) It does not presage your regulatory registration in the EU. The marks displayed in this application are given for information only and must be validated by the EU juries who will proceed with their verification, and any harmonization of marks between the various correctors of the tests. The purpose of this information is to help you situate yourself in relation to the expectations of the training and to allow your pedagogical referent teacher to guide you. In case of incorrect information, you must contact the teacher who entered the information.

  • ABINJ (Unjustified Absence)
  • ABJUS (justified absence)
  • PRST (PRESENT)
  • TNR (Work Not Rendered, so 0)
  • PPNOT (Can't Score): The teacher didn't give a score or there was nothing to score. The average calculation does not take this score into account.
  • NaN: The average cannot be calculated because there are missing marks.

The colors associated with the boxes are calculated according to the rank of the note in the group, a 14/20 is red if it is the worst note. Unless there are less than 10 notes or the teacher has chosen to color otherwise. If you disagree with an exam score, there is a one-day review of the scripts. No need to make an appointment with the teacher. There may be several days from when the note is entered until it becomes visible. This is due to the fact that the notes are entered over several days and that a harmonization must be done before their distribution.
