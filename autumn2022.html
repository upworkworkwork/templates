The marks displayed in this application are given for information only and must be validated by the EU juries who will proceed with their verification, and any harmonization of marks between the various correctors of the tests. The purpose of this information is to help you situate yourself in relation to the expectations of the training and to allow your pedagogical referent teacher to guide you. In case of incorrect information, you must contact the teacher who entered the information.
